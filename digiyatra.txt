Project Overview

DigiYatra

App Redesign

DigiYatra is a paperless airport experience used by
millions across India. The current home screen hides
the most essential travel detail - flight time - forcing
travelers to dig through email or WhatsApp. This
redesign rebuilds the boarding-pass surface around
glanceability, hierarchy, and calm, confident
composition.
